If you are interested in finding out a little more about me, below are the answers to a few questions you may have.

Why did you choose to become a Web Developer?

Well, a number of reasons really, but they all hover around a common goal. I spent the first half of my 20’s travelling around Oceania and Southeast Asia where I had some fantastic, unique experiences. I was using websites like helpx, couchsurfing and wwoof to seek out less travelled areas of these countries and was really blown away by the results. By far my favourite experiences were found this way, and it gave me the opportunity to experiment with a money-free lifestyle. It has become a goal of mine to create something that allows even more people to enjoy this way of living, and I believe I can achieve this through Web Development.

What kind of projects do you like to work on?

Anything that is aiming to help in some way. Helping people, animals, the environment. If the client has a positive message they wish to spread, like Ducky, then I am happy to work with them.

How did you learn to program?

Over quite a few years, I followed various free courses online, such as Free Code Camp and Code School. They got me to a certain level, but it wasn't until I completed my course at Career Foundry that I really felt like a true programmer.
